Safety First

We prioritize your security. While every user is verified, following these tips ensures a safe and positive experience.

🚨

In an Emergency?

If you ever feel unsafe or in immediate danger, verify your surroundings and contact campus security or local police immediately.

Safety Guidelines

Before the Meetup

  • In-App Only: Chat exclusively within NearSwap. Don't share phone numbers early.
  • Profile Check: Verify the user's badge and mutual connections.
  • Clear Terms: Agree on price, condition, and spot beforehand.

During the Exchange

  • Public Spots: Meet in busy campus areas (library, union, coffee shop).
  • Bring a Friend: Safety in numbers—bring a roommate along.
  • Inspect First: Check the item thoroughly before paying.

After the Swap

  • Leave a Review: Build trust by rating your experience.
  • Report Issues: Flag any suspicious behavior immediately.
  • Digital Payment: Stick to Venmo/Zelle for records.
