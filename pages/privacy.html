Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring a safe campus marketplace.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number for account authentication via WhatsApp OTP.
  • Profile Information: Name and profile picture (optional).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as listings viewed, messages sent, and search history.
  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers).
  • Location Data: We may collect information about your actual location to facilitate local meetups, subject to your permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity via WhatsApp OTP.
  • To facilitate transactions and connect buyers with sellers.
  • To improve our services and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you, including sending service-related notices.
  • To protect our users and preventing fraud or abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

The app and website may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or provide analytics. Links to privacy policies of third-party providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Google Analytics (website)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (WhatsApp Cloud API)

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your account directly within the app settings by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account.

Alternatively, you can request data deletion by contacting us at nearswap@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

5. Children’s Privacy

NearSwap is available to users of all ages, including students under the age of 13. However, we strictly require parental consent and supervision for any user under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at nearswap@gmail.com.
