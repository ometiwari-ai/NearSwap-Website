Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring a safe campus marketplace.

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

At NearSwap, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity: Name and email (via Google Sign-In)
  • Contact: Phone number (optional, for WhatsApp communication)
  • Location: Approximate location (only with your permission)
  • Content: Images and item descriptions you upload
  • Usage Data: Interactions such as listings viewed or messages sent
  • Device Info: Device type, OS version

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Enable buying/selling interactions
  • Show nearby listings
  • Improve app features
  • Send important notifications
  • Prevent fraud and misuse

4. Third-Party Services

We use several third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase
  • Cloudinary
  • WhatsApp

These services may collect data as per their own policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data when required to run the app or comply with laws.

6. Data Retention & Deletion
