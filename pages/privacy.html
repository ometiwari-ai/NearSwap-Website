NearSwap Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring a safe campus marketplace.

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

At NearSwap, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our app or website. By using NearSwap, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide NearSwap's services:

  • Identity: Name and email address (provided via Google Sign-In)
  • Contact: Phone number (optional — only used to open WhatsApp when another user taps the contact button on your listing; NearSwap does not send this number to WhatsApp's servers or any third party)
  • Location: Approximate location — collected only while you are actively using the app and only after you grant permission. Location is never collected in the background.
  • Content: Images and item descriptions that you upload for your listings
  • Usage Data: Interactions within the app such as listings viewed, messages sent, and searches performed
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version (used for compatibility and debugging only)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Enable buying and selling interactions between users
  • Display nearby listings based on your approximate location
  • Improve app features and performance
  • Send important account and service notifications
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and misuse of the platform
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate NearSwap. Each handles data according to its own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In — Used for user authentication.
  • Firebase (Google) — Used for database storage, cloud messaging, and app performance monitoring. Data may be stored on servers outside India.
  • Cloudinary — Used to store and serve images you upload. Data may be stored on servers outside India.
  • WhatsApp — NearSwap does not integrate WhatsApp's SDK or share any data with WhatsApp. When you tap a seller's contact button, your device opens WhatsApp directly using the seller's phone number. This is a device-level action; NearSwap does not transmit any data to WhatsApp.

5. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Some third-party services we use (such as Firebase and Cloudinary) may store or process your data on servers located outside India. By using NearSwap, you consent to this transfer. We ensure that any such transfer is subject to appropriate safeguards consistent with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers listed above, solely to operate the app
  • When required to comply with applicable law, court order, or government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of NearSwap, our users, or the public

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Account Deletion (App or Website)

You can delete your account at any time in Settings > Delete Account within the NearSwap app, or through our website. Upon deletion, your account and all associated personal data are permanently and completely deleted with no data retained after deletion.

Contact for Deletion

You may also request deletion by emailing us at nearswap@gmail.com. We will process your request within 48 hours.

8. Children's Privacy

NearSwap is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at nearswap@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We take reasonable and appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Role-based access controls to limit who can access your data
  • Use of trusted, security-audited third-party infrastructure (Firebase, Cloudinary)

No system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

10. Your Rights

Under applicable laws including India's DPDP Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Delete your personal data (see Section 7)
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at nearswap@gmail.com.

11. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For location access, optional phone number, and notifications
  • Contractual necessity: To provide the buying/selling features of NearSwap
  • Legitimate interest: To prevent fraud and improve app performance
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via in-app notification or email at least 7 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of NearSwap after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
