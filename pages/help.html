Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get verified?

To verify your account, sign up with your valid .edu email address. We will send a confirmation link to that inbox. Once clicked, your "Student Verified" badge will appear.

Is NearSwap free to use?

Yes! NearSwap is completely free to download and use. We do not charge listing fees for students.

How do I delete a listing?

Go to your "My Items" tab, tap the three dots on the item you want to remove, and select "Mark as Sold" or "Delete".

What if the buyer doesn't show up?

If a buyer ghosts you, you can report them in the chat. We recommend waiting 10-15 minutes and sending a message before leaving.

Can I trade items instead of selling?

Absolutely! NearSwap encourages swapping. Just select "Open to Offers/Trades" when you create your listing.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with account issues, reports, or technical bugs.
